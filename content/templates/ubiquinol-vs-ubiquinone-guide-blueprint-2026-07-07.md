## B. Search intent

Users comparing the two common CoQ10 forms likely want:

1. **Chemical/form distinction** — oxidized (ubiquinone) vs reduced (ubiquinol) at a high level
2. **Label clarity** — how each form appears on Supplement Facts
3. **Dose per serving** — mg CoQ10 per serving for example products
4. **Softgel/capsule format** — oil-based delivery, capsule material
5. **Price per 100 mg** — ubiquinol often costs more per mg — dated manual prices
6. **Testing/transparency** — third-party verification where applicable
7. **Absorption/bioavailability claims** — brand marketing vs conservative context only
8. **Added ingredients** — vitamin E, BioPerine, combo actives
9. **Allergen/oil base** — soybean oil, gelatin softgels

**Intent we do not serve:** heart-disease treatment advice, statin medication guidance, fertility/migraine/Parkinson's outcomes, guaranteed energy, "which form is medically better for you."

## F. Comparison criteria

| Criterion | Ubiquinone (typical label direction) | Ubiquinol (typical label direction) | Source requirement |
|-----------|-----------------------------------|-------------------------------------|-------------------|
| **Form naming on label** | "Coenzyme Q10 (ubiquinone)" or similar | "Ubiquinol" or "active form" — verify exact wording | Official Supplement Facts |
| **CoQ10 mg per serving** | e.g., 100–200 mg (verify locked SKU) | e.g., 100–200 mg (verify locked SKU) | Official label |
| **Softgels per serving** | Often 1–2 | Often 1–2 | Official label |
| **Carrier oil** | Often disclosed in softgels | Often disclosed in softgels | Official label |
| **Capsule material** | Gelatin vs veggie | Gelatin vs veggie | Official label |
| **Added ingredients** | Vitamin E common | Vitamin E, BioPerine in some SKUs | Official label |
| **Price per 100 mg** | Dated manual price | Dated manual price — often higher | Brand or retailer — not Amazon live price |
| **Testing / transparency** | USP/NSF if applicable | USP/NSF if applicable | Program database or COA |
| **Brand absorption claims** | Usually fewer | Often more — mirror as `brand_claim` only | Brand page |
| **Claim risk** | Generally lower marketing load | Higher "active form" / absorption marketing | Brand pages — conservative mirror |
