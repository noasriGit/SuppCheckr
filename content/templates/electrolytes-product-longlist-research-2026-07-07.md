## B. Source rules used

### Acceptable source hierarchy

1. **Official Nutrition Facts / Supplement Facts** (brand product page, label gallery, or official PDF)
2. **Official brand product page** (marketing tagged as `brand_claim`, not label verification)
3. **Certification databases** (NSF Certified for Sport, Informed Sport) matched to locked SKU
4. **Reliable retailer labels** — dated manual price and fallback label only when official label unavailable (caveat documented)

### Not used as primary evidence

- Amazon reviews, Q&A, star ratings, or product images
- Influencer or unsourced medical/hydration claims
- Live Amazon prices
- Pet/veterinary electrolyte sources
- Competitor comparison blogs as primary label evidence

**Amazon note:** Exact ASIN/UPC mapping **deferred** to Phase 14C+; no outbound links activated in Phase 14B.

## I. Electrolyte profile notes

### Sodium (primary differentiator)

| Range | Examples |
|-------|----------|
| **High (650–1,000+ mg)** | LMNT (1,000), Re-Lyte (810), Pedialyte Sport (650), BIOLYTE RTD (700) |
| **Moderate (300–500 mg)** | Liquid IV (500), Skratch (400), Thorne Catalyte (485), Nuun (300) |
| **Lower (<300 mg)** | Ultima (55), Kaged Hydra-Charge (115), Propel (260), Gatorade Zero (270), Cure (240) |

### Potassium

- Highest shortlist disclosure: **Ultima (250 mg)** despite low sodium.
- Liquid IV (370 mg) and LMNT (200 mg) bracket sugar vs sugar-free stick comparison.
- Nuun tablet includes **Cl (40 mg)** — useful transparency differentiator.

### Magnesium

- Disclosed on LMNT (60), Ultima (100), Nuun (25), Thorne (40), Skratch (50).
- **Not disclosed** on US Liquid I.V. Hydration Multiplier Nutrition Facts — document as absent/unknown, not zero.

### Sugar / carbs / calories

| Profile | Products |
|---------|----------|
| Zero sugar / zero cal | LMNT, Ultima, Re-Lyte |
| Sugar-containing sticks | Liquid IV (11 g sugar, 45 cal) |
| Low sugar tablet | Nuun (1 g sugar, 15 cal) |
| Sugar sports drink mix | Skratch (19 g sugar, 80 cal) |
| Low sugar sport powder | Thorne Catalyte (<1 g sugar, 30 cal) |

### Sweeteners

- **Sugar primary:** Liquid IV (cane sugar + dextrose), Skratch (cane + dextrose)
- **Stevia / monk fruit:** LMNT, Ultima, Nuun (stevia + small dextrose)
- **Artificial:** Propel, Gatorade Zero (sucralose + ace-K)
