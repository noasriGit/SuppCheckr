## Site overview

SuppCheckr is an informational supplement comparison and review website that helps consumers evaluate products by label transparency, dosage clarity, third-party testing, ingredient quality, additives, price/value, and editorial conservatism. The site publishes human-written reviews and comparison tables for Creatine, Magnesium, and Vitamin C supplements. SuppCheckr does not sell supplements directly, does not operate user accounts or checkout, and does not provide medical advice. Product images are branded placeholders — not Amazon or manufacturer photography. Amazon customer reviews, star ratings, Q&A, and live prices are not used as editorial evidence.
